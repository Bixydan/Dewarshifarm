Want to reach us? We are just 9 km away from Highway

Route 1: There is basically 3 routes to this farm. First, you will have to go Damauli. From there start your trail towards Buddha Singh Rajmarga (Towards Ghumaune). On the way, you'll find Sode. Cross the bridge and follow road in your 4*4 vehicle. towards Chipchipe.

Route 2: Come to Ghumaune, cross the suspension bridge and follow the Buddha Singh rajmarga till sarangghat. Again cross the suspension bridge towards Shree Bhrikuti Ma Vi. Then follow the road towards Chipchipe. OR, instead of Sarangghat, come to Naldi or Dovan then cross the suspension bridge to get to Chipchipe

Route 3: Come to Gaighat, cross the suspension bridge and hop on the public bolero and to chipchipe.
